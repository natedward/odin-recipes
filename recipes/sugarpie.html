Sugar Pie

A delicious sugar pie

A sugar pie just a bunch of sugar in pie. Thats it.

Ingredients

  • pie crust
  • sugar
  • maple syrup
  • butterscotch

Steps

  1. place sugar in pie crust
  2. pour maple syrup in pie crust
  3. pour butterscotch in pie crust
  4. place pie in oven at 450 degrees celsius for 20 minutes
