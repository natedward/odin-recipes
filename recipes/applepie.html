Apple Pie

A beautiful baked apple pie

This ain't you're grandmothers apple pie, this apple pie with change everything you know about apples.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Pie crust
  • caramel chips

Steps

  1. Peel and cut apple in slices
  2. Put apple slices in pie crust
  3. Pour caramel chips over apple slices
  4. Place entire pie in freezer for 24 hours
